# SlingPlan
<br/>
SlingPlan allows you to quickly schedule your events, find the vendors you need, and negotiate pricing directly. Our intuitive map allows you to explore and bookmark vendors by services and location. Creating your event using our step-by-step process painlessly puts you in contact with the best-suited vendors.
